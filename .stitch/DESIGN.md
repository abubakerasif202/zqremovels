# ZQ Removals Design System: Luxury Editorial

## 1. Core Visual Identity
- **Style:** Luxury Editorial (think high-end architecture or lifestyle magazines).
- **Vibe:** Disciplined, precise, established, and premium.
- **Goal:** Shift from "moving company" to "logistics and relocation partner."
- **Emotional Layer:** Warm, reassuring, and professional. The "human touch" within the discipline.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary (Navy):** `#0A192F` (Deep, high-trust navy for backgrounds and headings).
- **Secondary (Gold):** `#B89764` (Subtle gold/brass for accents, eyebrows, and CTA highlights).
- **Base (Ivory):** `#FDFCF8` (Off-white/ivory for main backgrounds to feel softer than pure white).
- **Accent (Mist):** `rgba(10, 25, 47, 0.03)` (A very soft navy wash for secondary sections).

## 3. Typography
- **Headings:** High-contrast Serif (e.g., "Fraunces" or "Playfair Display"). Large, tight tracking for hero h1.
- **Body:** Clean, legible Sans-Serif (e.g., "Inter" or "Manrope"). Slightly increased line height for readability.
- **Eyebrows:** All-caps, tracked-out Sans-Serif in Gold.

## 4. Layout & Spacing
- **Rhythm:** Generous whitespace (luxury = space).
- **Grid:** Asymmetric editorial layouts. Hero should feel open.
- **Borders:** Thin, elegant lines (1px) in light navy or subtle gold.
- **Dynamic Rhythm:** Alternating text/image sections with layered overlaps to break grid fatigue.

## 5. Components
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Navy background, Gold text or border.
  - Secondary: Ghost button with 1px border.
- **Cards:** Subtle depth (soft shadows) or minimal outlines. Use visual markers (refined icons or serif accents).
- **Media:** Overlapping layouts, layered depth, and large immersive backgrounds. Mix narrative text with atmospheric photography.

## 6. Design System Notes for Stitch Generation
> **USE THIS SECTION IN STITCH PROMPTS**
> 
> Redesign using a **Luxury Editorial Style** with **Emotional Richness**:
> - **Palette:** Navy (`#0A192F`), Gold (`#B89764`), and Ivory (`#FDFCF8`).
> - **Typography:** Elegant Serifs for headings, modern Sans-Serif for body. Gold eyebrows.
