Before you book

The North Adelaide move checklist that usually keeps inner-city quotes accurate.

The cleanest inner-north quotes come from clear access information. Most pricing surprises in North Adelaide are caused by parking, stairs, or carrying conditions that were never confirmed early enough.

1

Property and street access

Confirm whether each end is a terrace, apartment, townhouse, or office, then explain the stopping position, frontage, and any parking or loading limits.

Stairs and carrying distance
