Materials Matter

Premium Packing Materials (Heavy-duty boxes, custom crating for art)

Premium packing starts with the right materials and the right protection level for the actual inventory. Fragile kitchens, framed art, mirrors, designer furniture, and electronics should not be treated like standard storage-box freight.

Heavy-Duty Materials for Everyday Inventory

Stronger cartons, layered wrapping, and room-by-room labeling keep the job faster to load and easier to unpack correctly.

  • Double-walled box options
  • Dish, glassware, and wardrobe packing
  • Padding for lamps, décor, and media units

Custom Protection for Fragile and High-Value Pieces

Art, mirrors, antiques, and unusually shaped pieces need a more deliberate packing standard so they can travel with less risk.

  • Custom crating for artwork when required
  • Mirror and glass panel reinforcement
  • Soft-wrap layers for delicate finishes
Service Levels

Partial vs. Full-House Packing: Choose Your Level of Care

Some clients only want kitchens, artwork, and breakables handled. Others want a full-home service that removes almost all packing pressure before move day. We scope the service level clearly so labour, materials, and timing stay aligned.

Option 02

Most-of-House Packing

Ideal for families balancing work, kids, and a fixed move date who want the bulk of the home professionally prepared.

The Valet Unpack: Settle In on Day One

Valet unpacking turns the destination from a box-filled handover into a usable home faster. Kitchen zones, bedrooms, and daily-use essentials are unpacked in the right order so you can settle in immediately instead of living around cartons for the next week.

Packing FAQ

Common questions before booking premium packing support.

These answers cover the decisions people usually want to make before they book packing support, valet unpacking, or a higher-care protection standard.

Do I need full-house packing to use the service?

No. Many clients book targeted packing just for fragile items, kitchens, artwork, or rooms that are difficult to prepare on their own.

Can packing be combined with an interstate move?

Yes. In fact, longer routes usually benefit the most from a stronger packing standard because the load will spend longer in transit and may involve more complex delivery coordination.

What should I include in the packing quote request?

Include the property type, move date, suburb route, whether you want partial or full packing, and any fragile, oversized, or high-value pieces that need a higher protection standard.
