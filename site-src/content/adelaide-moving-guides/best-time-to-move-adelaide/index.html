Move timing guide

Best time to move in Adelaide.

Weekdays usually give more flexibility, while weekends and month-end windows are often tighter and more expensive to secure.

Use this guide when you are choosing a booking date around settlement, lease handover, or building access.

01

Weekdays

Usually easier for access, parking, and broader crew availability.

02

End of month

Often the busiest period because leases and settlements cluster together.

03

Building windows

If a tower or office controls the lift schedule, that access window should decide the date.
