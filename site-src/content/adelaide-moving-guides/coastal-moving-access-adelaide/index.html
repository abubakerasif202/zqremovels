Before quote review

What coastal moves usually need clarified early.

A short coastal move can still be access-heavy. If the truck cannot stand close to the property, the route includes stairs, or the job runs through a shared apartment entry, the labour plan changes immediately. This is why coastal access should be treated as its own planning layer before the move is priced.

01

Map where the truck can actually stand

Parking rules, loading restrictions, and busier frontage can change where the crew starts carrying from.

02

Separate the delicate and bulky pieces

Coastal stairs, shared entries, and tighter frontage make furniture handling more sensitive, especially for glass, timber, and oversized items.

03

Decide whether the route is purely local

Some coastal jobs stay local. Others become a southern corridor transfer or feed into an interstate handover, which makes packing and timing more important.
