Move timing guide

How long moves take in Adelaide.

Move duration depends on access, inventory, packing, and whether the route is local, CBD-led, or interstate.

Use this guide to set expectations before you book a move window.

01

Apartments

Lift bookings, corridor rules, and shared access usually make apartment moves slower than the room count suggests.

02

Family homes

Room count matters, but garage volume, whitegoods, and carrying distance often matter more.

03

Office and interstate

Business restarts and longer linehaul routes need more sequencing and usually need cleaner preparation.
