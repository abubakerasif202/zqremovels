Damage prevention guide

Avoiding damage during an Adelaide move.

Damage risk falls when wrapping, access planning, and load sequencing are handled before the truck arrives.

Use Furniture Removalists Adelaide for bulky items and Packing Services Adelaide for higher-risk rooms.

01

Wrap early

Fragile items, finishes, and corners should be protected before load day begins.

02

Clear the path

Stairs, turns, and carry routes should be checked for tight points, cords, and clutter.

03

Sequence the load

Heavy items and delicate items should not fight for the same position in the truck.
