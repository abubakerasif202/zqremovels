Before quote review

The CBD office details that should be confirmed early.

A city move becomes easier when the building rules are treated as part of the inventory brief. The office may only have ten desks, but if the dock is booked for one hour, the service lift is restricted, and security wants a named contractor list, the move needs a tighter plan than a standard suburban office.
