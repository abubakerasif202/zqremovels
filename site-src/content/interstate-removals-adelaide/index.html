Interstate FAQ

Common questions before booking interstate removals from Adelaide

How far in advance should I book an interstate move?

Two to four weeks is ideal for most moves. Earlier is better during peak periods or when building bookings are required.

Do you provide fixed-price interstate quotes?

Yes. Quotes are prepared after we review route details, access conditions, and inventory so scope is clear before booking.

Can you move fragile and high-value items interstate?

Yes. We plan specific wrapping and handling requirements for fragile or high-value items as part of the booking brief.

What details should I include in the quote request?

Include pickup and delivery suburbs, preferred dates, property access notes, inventory size, and any special handling requirements.

Do you only service Melbourne and Sydney routes?

No. Those are common routes, but we also quote custom interstate destinations based on route and delivery requirements.
