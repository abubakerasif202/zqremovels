Commercial FAQ

Questions business clients ask before approving a relocation.

These answers focus on the operational details that usually decide whether a commercial move brief is ready to approve.

Can you move our office after hours or on a weekend?

Yes. Many Adelaide commercial relocations are scheduled for evenings or weekends so teams can keep working through the day and return to a reset space.

Do you handle monitors, IT equipment, and workstation resets?

Yes. We scope screens, docks, workstations, archives, and shared equipment separately so they can be protected, transported, and rebuilt in a more controlled sequence.

What information should be included in a commercial move brief?

Include both addresses, preferred move window, staff or desk count, building restrictions, dock or lift bookings, and the departments or equipment that need to be operational first.
