Packing FAQ

Frequently asked questions about booking packing services in Adelaide

Do I need to book a full pack to use your packing service?

No. You can book targeted support for fragile rooms, selected furniture, or specific high-risk items.

Can packing be included with interstate moves?

Yes. Interstate clients often choose higher packing standards because items spend longer in transit.

What packing materials do you use?

We use heavy-duty cartons, protective wrapping systems, and reinforced methods suited to item type and transport profile.

Do you offer valet unpacking at destination?

Yes. Valet unpacking can prioritise key rooms first to help the property feel operational sooner.

What details should I include when requesting a packing quote?

Include property type, move date, service level, and any fragile or oversized pieces needing special handling.
