Packing FAQ

Common questions before booking premium packing support.

These answers cover the decisions people usually want to make before they book packing support, valet unpacking, or a higher-care protection standard.

Do I need full-house packing to use the service?

No. Many clients book targeted packing just for fragile items, kitchens, artwork, or rooms that are difficult to prepare on their own.

Can packing be combined with an interstate move?

Yes. In fact, longer routes usually benefit the most from a stronger packing standard because the load will spend longer in transit and may involve more complex delivery coordination.

What should I include in the packing quote request?

Include the property type, move date, suburb route, whether you want partial or full packing, and any fragile, oversized, or high-value pieces that need a higher protection standard.
