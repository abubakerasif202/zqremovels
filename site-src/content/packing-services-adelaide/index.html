Packing FAQ

Common questions before booking packing help.

These answers cover the decisions people usually want to make before adding packing support to a move.

Do I need full packing to use the service?

No. Many customers only book packing for fragile items, the kitchen, wardrobes, or the rooms that would take the most time on their own.

Can packing be combined with an interstate move?

Yes. Longer routes usually benefit from a stronger packing standard because the items are travelling further and need to stay protected for longer.

What should I include in the packing quote request?

Include the property type, the move date, the rooms you want packed, and any fragile or bulky items that need extra care.
