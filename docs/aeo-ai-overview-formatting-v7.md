# AEO And AI Overview Formatting V7

## Goal

Format priority answers so search engines and AI answer systems can identify direct, helpful responses before the supporting detail.

## Pages Affected

- Cost pages
- Moving quote pages
- Fixed-price pages
- Guide pages
- Interstate route pages
- Office removals pages

## Exact Implementation Rules

- Use a question-style H2 or H3.
- Put the direct answer in the first sentence of the next paragraph.
- Keep the answer short, then add one or two supporting sentences.
- Add visible FAQ only when the page has real FAQ content.
- Emit FAQPage schema only when visible FAQ exists.

## Unsafe Claims To Avoid

- No invented prices.
- No unsupported guarantees.
- No fake credentials.
- No review or rating claims in schema.
- No destination-office claims on interstate routes.

## Follow-Up Actions

- Monitor Search Console for question-style impressions.
- Expand AEO blocks only where they answer real pre-quote questions.

## Owner Confirmation Required

- Exact prices, if the business chooses to publish them later.
- Insurance wording if an answer needs cover details.
