## Recommended UTM patterns

### GBP website link

- `utm_source=google`
- `utm_medium=organic`
- `utm_campaign=gbp`
- `utm_content=website`

### GBP posts

- `utm_source=google`
- `utm_medium=organic`
- `utm_campaign=gbp`
- `utm_content=post`

### Citation links

- `utm_source=<platform>`
- `utm_medium=citation`
- `utm_campaign=local_seo`
- `utm_content=profile_link`

### Partner backlinks

- `utm_source=<partner>`
- `utm_medium=referral`
- `utm_campaign=local_partnership`
- `utm_content=resource_link`
