# Interstate Route Expansion V7

## Goal

Capture route-specific interstate search intent while clearly presenting ZQ Removals as an Adelaide-origin removalist, not a fake local-office operator in NSW or VIC.

## Pages Affected

- `/adelaide-to-sydney-removalists/`
- `/adelaide-to-melbourne-removalists/`

## Exact Implementation Rules

- Phrase every route as interstate removals from Adelaide to the destination.
- Include one H1, canonical apex URL, visible FAQ, Service schema, and Breadcrumb schema.
- Include route-specific sections: who the route is for, planning considerations, Adelaide pickup planning, destination delivery planning, inventory and packing notes, and quote factors.
- Include subject-to-availability wording for route dates.
- For Smithfield NSW, clearly distinguish it from Smithfield SA and Smithfield Plains.
