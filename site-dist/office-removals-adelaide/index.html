Move planning

Plan the office move around the business day and the building rules.

A commercial move is easier to manage when the inventory, building rules, and restart priorities are clear before the first desk is moved.

Step 01

Review the office brief

Confirm the address, team size, workstation count, shared furniture, and the parts of the business that need to come back online first.

  • Floor-by-floor or zone-by-zone planning
  • Loading docks, lifts, and access rules checked early
  • Preferred timing captured before the quote is finalised
Step 02

Set the move window

Once the access rules are clear, we can plan the move window around the working day, the building, and the handover requirements.

  • Loading and unloading windows reviewed
  • Communication matched to site contacts
  • Staged moves possible when the brief requires it
Step 03

Unload by team or priority area

Delivery order can be matched to the rooms, teams, or equipment you need ready first at the new site.

  • Room and desk labels carried through the move
  • Shared equipment placed in the right sequence
  • Final handover checked before sign-off
Equipment and files

Sensitive equipment, workstations, and files handled with care.

Offices often include monitors, docks, files, archives, printers, treatment-room items, or stock that need a clearer plan than a standard furniture move.

Workstations kept together

Desks, screens, accessories, and related equipment can be grouped by team or area so the new site is easier to reset.

  • Label-led disassembly and reloading
  • Monitors and accessories separated properly
  • Shared equipment identified before the move

Files, stock, and fragile equipment planned early

Stock rooms, archives, treatment spaces, and shared equipment need the handling sequence worked out before move day.

  • Files and stock grouped by area
  • Fragile items flagged in the quote brief
  • Packing support available for sensitive equipment
Commercial scope

Moves sized to the office brief.

Some office moves are straightforward. Others involve multiple rooms, shared access areas, or staged delivery. The quote should reflect the brief, not force it into a generic template.

Single-site office moves

For offices, clinics, studios, and workspaces moving from one address to another within Adelaide.

  • Desks, chairs, monitors, and files
  • Shared-area furniture and equipment
  • Loading windows and placement order reviewed early

Staged or mixed commercial moves

For moves that include storage, multiple rooms, or a staged handover across more than one area.

  • Internal office reshuffles or staged departures
  • Clinic, retail, and mixed-use workspaces
  • Interstate planning available if another city is involved

Commercial FAQ

Questions business clients ask before approving a move.

These answers focus on access, timing, and the information needed to quote an Adelaide office move properly.

Can you move our office over a weekend to avoid disrupting business?

Yes. After-hours and weekend office moves are available. We schedule commercial moves around your business hours so your team arrives Monday morning at a fully set-up new office.

Do you handle IT equipment like computers, monitors, and printers?

Yes. We handle standard office IT equipment including desktop computers, monitors, and printers. We recommend your IT team handle server disconnection, but we transport all equipment safely.

How much does an office removal in Adelaide cost?

Office removal costs depend on the size of your premises, volume of items, number of crew required, and timing. We provide a detailed quote after reviewing the brief. Contact us to arrange a consultation.

Do you provide packing services for office moves?

Yes. Our team can pack your entire office prior to move day, including desks, storage areas, and kitchen items. Packing is quoted separately.

Are you insured for commercial moves?

Yes. ZQ Removals carries full insurance for commercial removal jobs. We can provide proof of insurance if required by your building manager.

What Adelaide business areas do you service?

We service the Adelaide CBD, North Adelaide, Norwood, Prospect, Port Adelaide, Thebarton, Mile End, Marion, and all metropolitan Adelaide suburbs.
