# Internal Linking Plan

## Implemented Linking Rules

### Homepage

- Homepage now links directly to:
  - local removals
  - interstate removals
  - office removals
  - furniture removals
  - packing services
  - contact / quote page
  - Adelaide CBD
  - Marion
  - Salisbury
  - Elizabeth
  - Glenelg
  - Adelaide moving guides

### Core Service Pages

- Local removals page links into top suburb pages and quote flow
- Interstate hub links into Melbourne and Sydney route pages plus quote flow
- Office, furniture, and packing pages now have stronger service-specific CTA copy
- Shared generator now appends related-link sections for service pages so each one can pass users into:
  - local removals
  - interstate removals
  - packing or furniture support where relevant
  - contact / quote page

### Suburb Pages

- Priority suburb pages now link back to:
  - `/removalists-adelaide/`
  - `/contact-us/#quote-form`
  - relevant commercial pages such as office or packing services where context fits
- Shared generator also appends a related-links section to suburb pages for:
  - Adelaide local removals hub
  - furniture removals
  - packing services
  - contact / quote page

### Route Pages

- Route pages link back to:
  - interstate removals hub
  - quote page
- Shared generator appends related links for:
  - interstate hub
  - local Adelaide removals
  - packing services
  - contact / quote page

### Guide Pages

- Shared generator now appends commercial next-step links to the guide hub and guide articles
- Guide pages now feed users into:
  - local removals
  - interstate removals
  - office removals
  - contact / quote page

## Anchor Text Approach

- Use natural anchors such as:
  - `Adelaide local removals`
  - `interstate removals Adelaide`
  - `packing services Adelaide`
  - `request a fixed-price quote`
  - suburb names when the suburb is the destination page
- Avoid repeated exact-match anchors jammed into the same section
- Keep anchors customer-facing and action-oriented

## Why This Matters

- Reduces crawl dead ends on suburb and guide pages
- Moves users from informational content into commercial quote paths
- Makes service relationships clearer without stuffing keywords into paragraphs
